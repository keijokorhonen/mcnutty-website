Summary

In physics, classical mechanics concerns the set of physical laws that govern the motion of bodies under the influence of forces. It describes the motion of macroscopic objects, from tennis balls to solar systems.

Contents

2.1 - Motion

What you need to know:

  • Understand distance, displacement, speed, velocity and acceleration
  • Analyse graphs of motion
  • Solve motion problems using 'SUVAT' equations
  • Understand projectile motion
  • Understand the effects of fluid resistance force on motion (air resistance) and terminal speed

Definitions:

Term Symbol Definition
Distance d Length of path followed
Displacement s Change in position
Average velocity v Change in position over a time period
Acceleration a Rate of change of velocity
